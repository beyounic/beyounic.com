Beyounic SA is a Swiss-based company. Our principal investor is Agire Invest. Our mission is to make event organizer's lives easier, simpler, and more enjoyable.

Ohanah is an event management platform from Beyounic: open source, self hostable and available in 38 languages.

Since it's launch in 2012, Ohanah has been used by thousands to create more than a million events.
