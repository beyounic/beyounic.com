Beyounic SA is a Swiss-based company. Our principal investor is Agire Invest. Our mission is to make event organizer's lives easier, simpler, and more enjoyable.

Ohanah is an open platform from beyounic. In other words, ohanah is: all you need to manage events online, open source, self hosted and available in 38 languages.

The new Ohanah launched in 2012. It is used by thousands and it has created more then a milion events.
